Who We Are

The Central Post is committed to providing high-quality, engaging content for those who are looking for an up to date analysis of politics, business, and sports. The Central Post does not endorse any political party or support a particular political agenda. Each article reflects the thoughts, opinions, and conjectures of the author and is not to be taken as a statement of fact or represent the opinion of the organization.

Meet The Founder

My name is Tony, I recently graduated, in the spring of the pandemic, with a degree in Business and Politics. After spending the past several months witnessing the divisive rhetoric and anger-fueled events, I decided to create a place for those looking to stay up to date on the newest events while escaping all of the partisan noise. The Central Post is meant to be a platform for nuanced, balanced, and thoughtful analysis that comes from years of researching and learning about the effects of various political and economic events on our society. If you have any questions about the platform or thoughts on how improve the organization, please reach out to me using the "Contact Us" page.
